Planalytics is a SaaS company that helps its clients understand the impact of weather on retail demand. Planalytics uses advanced analytics and machine learning to understand how a given day's weather will impact demand for each SKU in each store for national brands. Planalytics then plugs these insights into existing ERP systems in order to improve inventory forecasts and reduce waste and shrink. A core benefit of Planalytic's technology is waste reduction for their grocery store clients as improved forecasting leads to less waste.

Working directly under CEO Fred Fox, my role was to help Planalytics and their clients understand the associated carbon emission reduction stemming from the waste reduction. This would allow Planalytics to market to ESG groups inside large corporations and demonstrate an additional, non-monetary benefit of their technology. In order to bring this idea from ideation to development to deployment, my team and I had to:

  • Understand and pick one of the many different methodologies companies, governments, and scientists use to track carbon emissions.
  • Create our own quantitative algorithm to convert Planaytics' waste saving metrics into carbon emissions reduction figures.
